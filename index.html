Algebra 2 Overview

Algebra 2 builds on the foundations of Algebra 1, diving deeper into more complex functions, equations, and mathematical concepts. This course will cover everything from quadratic functions and logarithms to matrices and conic sections, giving you a powerful set of tools to solve advanced problems.

Course Session Summary

  • We start with a review of functions and their graphs.
  • We'll then move into advanced topics like quadratic and polynomial functions, rational expressions, and radicals.
  • The second half of the course focuses on exponential and logarithmic functions, matrices, and sequences.
  • We finish with an introduction to probability and a comprehensive final review.
